ABOUT US

In 2021, Papa Potato was founded with a simple goal: Share our great tasting potato chips with snack lovers. In keeping with the goal, Papa Potato offers consumers various types of chips - including thick cut and wavy potato chips - in over 10 flavours. The potato chips are made with quality potatoes to satisfy the cravings of consumers.

Simple, tasty and real.
